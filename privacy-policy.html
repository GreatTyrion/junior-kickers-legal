Information Collection and Storage

+

What We DON'T Collect

Junior Kickers Journey does NOT collect or access:

  • Personal information (names, email addresses, phone numbers)
  • Account credentials (no user accounts exist)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers or tracking information
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic information
  • Payment or financial information
  • Social media profiles or connections
  • Contacts or address book data

What Stays on Your Device

All information you enter remains locally stored on your device:

  • Match records (opponents, scores, statistics, performance)
  • Skill assessments (ratings and progress)
  • Achievement data (badges earned, progress)
  • Media files (photos/videos in app's private storage)
  • App settings and preferences

When you attach photos or videos, the app creates optimized copies in its private storage. Original files in your gallery remain untouched. Photos are automatically compressed to save space. These copies are deleted when you remove them or uninstall the app.

Data Security

+

Local Device Security

Since all data is stored locally, security depends on your device's measures:

  • Device Encryption: Data uses iOS/Android system encryption
  • Device Lock: Protect with passcode, PIN, or biometric authentication

Our Security Approach

  • No network communication = no data interception risk
  • No user accounts = no account breach risk
  • No cloud storage = no cloud security concerns
  • No third-party services = no third-party vulnerabilities

Your Responsibility

To keep your data secure:

  • Use a strong device lock (passcode, biometric)
  • Keep your device's operating system updated
  • Don't share your device with unauthorized users
  • Back up your device regularly (optional)
