Introduction

Junior Kickers Journey ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles information when you use our service.

Key Point: Junior Kickers Journey is designed with privacy first. We do NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal data. All information you enter stays on your device.

Information Collection and Storage

What We DON'T Collect

Junior Kickers Journey does NOT collect or access:

  • Personal information (names, email addresses, phone numbers)
  • Account credentials (no user accounts exist)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers or tracking information
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic information
  • Payment or financial information
  • Social media profiles or connections
  • Contacts or address book data
  • Photos or media files from your device gallery (we create private copies of media you select)

What Stays on Your Device

All information you enter into Junior Kickers Journey remains locally stored on your device:

  • Player profiles (child's name, date of birth, positions)
  • Training session logs (dates, durations, notes, ratings)
  • Match records (opponents, scores, statistics, performance)
  • Skill assessments (ratings and progress)
  • Achievement data (badges earned, progress)
  • Media files (photos/videos copied to app's private storage with automatic compression)
  • App settings and preferences

Important: Media File Handling

When you attach photos or videos to activities, the app creates optimized copies in its private storage directory on your device. Original files in your device gallery remain untouched. Photos are automatically compressed to save storage space while maintaining quality. These copies are only accessible by the app and are deleted if you remove them or uninstall the app.

How We Use Information

Since all data stays on your device, we do not "use" your information in any way. You have complete control over:

  • Creating and editing player profiles
  • Logging training sessions and matches
  • Viewing progress and analytics
  • Managing achievements and media
  • Exporting reports (PDFs generated locally)
  • Deleting data (complete control)

All data processing happens locally on your device. No information is sent to our servers or any third parties.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT share your data with anyone because we never have access to your data.

  • No data transmission to our servers
  • No sharing with third parties
  • No selling of information
  • No advertising networks or trackers
  • No analytics services
  • No social media integration (unless you explicitly share via your device's native share function)

Your Sharing Choices

You may optionally choose to share information using your device's native sharing features:

  • Share achievement celebrations via social media, messaging, or email
  • Export PDF progress reports and share them with coaches, family, or others
  • Take screenshots and share them

These are YOUR choices. We do not automatically share anything on your behalf.

Data Security

Local Device Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, security depends on your device's security measures:

  • Device Encryption: Data is stored in your device's secure storage (utilizing iOS/Android system encryption)
  • Device Lock: Protect your device with a passcode, PIN, biometric authentication, or pattern lock
  • App Access: Optional app lock feature (future enhancement) for additional security

Our Security Measures

  • No network communication = no risk of data interception
  • No user accounts = no account breach risk
  • No cloud storage = no cloud security concerns
  • No third-party services = no third-party vulnerabilities

Your Responsibility

To keep your data secure:

  • Use a strong device lock (passcode, biometric)
  • Keep your device's operating system updated
  • Don't share your device with unauthorized users
  • Back up your device regularly (optional)

Children's Privacy

Junior Kickers Journey is designed for parents to track their children's soccer development (typically ages 6-12). We are committed to protecting children's privacy.

COPPA Compliance (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act)

Since our app:

  • Does NOT collect any personal information
  • Does NOT transmit any data online
  • Does NOT require registration or user accounts
  • Does NOT have social features or community interaction

We do NOT collect "personal information" from children as defined by COPPA. Parents have full control over all data entered into the app.

Parental Control

  • Parents control ALL data entry
  • Parents decide what information to record
  • Parents control data viewing and deletion
  • Parents decide if/when to share information externally

Data Retention and Deletion

How Long Data is Stored

Data remains on your device until YOU decide to delete it:

  • Individual activities (training sessions, matches) can be deleted anytime
  • Player profiles can be edited or deleted
  • Media attachments can be removed
  • The entire app and all data can be deleted by uninstalling the app

How to Delete Your Data

Delete Individual Items:

  • Navigate to the item (training session, match, player)
  • Use the delete function within the app

Delete All Data:

  • Uninstall the Junior Kickers Journey app from your device
  • This permanently removes all app data from your device

Important

Once deleted, data CANNOT be recovered (unless you have a device backup). We have no copies of your data.

Your Rights and Control

Since all data stays on your device, you have complete control:

You Have the Right To:

  • Access: View all your data anytime within the app
  • Modify: Edit any information you've entered
  • Delete: Remove any or all data at any time
  • Export: Generate PDF reports of your data
  • Control: Decide what to track and what to share
  • Privacy: Keep all information completely private

No Need to Request:

Since we never have access to your data, you don't need to request:

  • Data access (you already have full access)
  • Data correction (you can edit anytime)
  • Data deletion (you can delete anytime)
  • Data portability (export PDFs locally)

Important: We never collect, store, or have access to your financial or payment information under any circumstances.

Junior Kickers Journey requests minimal device permissions:

Permissions We Request:

Photo Library Access (Optional)

  • Purpose: To allow you to select photos/videos from your gallery to attach to activities
  • Usage: We only access photos/videos YOU specifically select
  • Storage: We create optimized copies of selected media in the app's private storage (photos are compressed, videos are copied as-is)

Permissions We DON'T Request:

  • Internet/Network access (app is offline-only)
  • Location services
  • Camera access (you use your device's camera app separately)
