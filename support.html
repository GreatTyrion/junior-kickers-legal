üöÄ Getting Started

How do I install the app?

Junior Kickers Journey is available on:

  • iOS: Download from the App Store (iOS 15.1+ required)
  • Android: Download from Google Play (Android 7.0+ required)

Simply search for "Junior Kickers Journey" in your app store and tap Install.

Do I need an account to use the app?

No! Junior Kickers Journey requires no user accounts, no registration, and no login. Simply download the app and start tracking your child's soccer journey.

How do I set up my child's profile?

When you first launch the app, you'll be guided through creating a player profile:

  1. Enter your child's name
  2. Select their date of birth
  3. Choose their primary soccer position(s)
  4. Tap "Create Profile"

That's it! You're ready to start tracking activities.

Can I track multiple children?

Yes! Junior Kickers Journey supports multiple players:

  • Go to Settings ‚Üí Add Player
  • Create additional profiles for each child
  • Switch between players anytime from Settings
  • Each player's data is kept separate and private

‚ú® Features & Usage

How do I log a training session?

Logging a training session takes less than 2 minutes:

  1. Navigate to the Training tab
  2. Tap "Add Training Session"
  3. Select the date and time
  4. Enter duration (e.g., 60 minutes)
  5. Choose activity type (Practice, Skills, Fitness, etc.)
  6. Select focus areas (optional)
  7. Rate performance (1-5 stars)
  8. Add notes (optional)
  9. Tap "Save"
How do I record a match?

Recording a match is simple:

  1. Navigate to the Training tab ‚Üí "Log Match"
  2. Select date and time
  3. Enter opponent name
  4. Select home/away
  5. Enter team and opponent scores
  6. Record your child's statistics (goals, assists, etc.)
  7. Select position(s) played
  8. Enter minutes played
  9. Rate performance
  10. Add notes (optional)
  11. Tap "Save"
How do I edit a training session or match?

You can edit any activity after logging it:

  1. Navigate to the Training tab or find the activity on your Dashboard
  2. Tap the activity card to open the Activity Detail screen
  3. Tap the "Edit Activity" button
  4. Make your changes to any fields
  5. Tap "Update Training Session" or "Update Match"

Note: The app will recalculate achievements based on the updated data. If an achievement no longer qualifies (e.g., reducing goals from 3 to 2 removes "Hat Trick Hero"), you'll be notified.

How do I add photos or videos to activities?

Adding media is optional and easy to do:

  1. Find the activity in the Training tab or on your Dashboard
  2. Tap the activity card to open the Activity Detail screen
  3. Tap "Add Media" button
  4. Choose "Take Photo/Video" or "Select from Gallery"
  5. Select or capture your media
  6. Media is automatically attached to the activity

Tip: Activities with attached media show a üì∑ icon on their cards, making them easy to identify at a glance.

Important: Photos are automatically compressed to save storage space while maintaining quality. Original files in your gallery remain unchanged.

What are achievements and how do I earn them?

Achievements celebrate your child's soccer milestones:

  • First Steps: Complete your first training session
  • First Goal: Score your first goal in a match
  • Hat Trick Hero: Score 3 goals in one match
  • Consistency Champion: Train 5 consecutive days in a row
  • Dedicated Player: Complete 10 training sessions
  • Time Investment: Log 50 hours of training
  • 30 Days Active: Log activity on 30 different days
  • Early Bird: Log training before 8 AM

And more! Check the Progress tab ‚Üí Awards section to see all available milestones and your progress.

What happens when I unlock an achievement?

When you unlock an achievement, you'll see a celebration with special animations:

  • Animated Celebration: Each achievement has unique visual effects based on its rarity
  • Instant Sharing: Tap "Share" to post your child's achievement to social media or messaging apps
  • Personalized Messages: Share messages include your child's name (e.g., "Emma just earned the 'First Goal' achievement!")
  • Multiple Achievements: If you unlock several at once, you'll see them one by one
  • Soccer Facts: After celebrations, you'll see a fun educational soccer fact!

You can also replay celebrations anytime from the Progress tab ‚Üí Awards section by tapping on earned achievements.

How do I share achievements?

There are two ways to share your child's achievements:

  1. During Celebration: When an achievement is unlocked, tap the "Share" button on the celebration screen
  2. From Awards Section: Go to Progress ‚Üí Awards, tap any earned achievement, tap "Replay Celebration", then tap "Share"

The app will create a personalized message with your child's name and the achievement details. You can share via any app on your device (Messages, WhatsApp, Facebook, email, etc.).

How do I view progress and statistics?

The Dashboard provides an overview of your child's soccer journey:

  • Quick Stats: Total sessions, matches, hours, and goals
  • Activity Timeline: Recent activities at a glance (tap any to see details)
  • Progress Tab: Detailed analytics, charts, and achievements
  • Activity Analytics: Training and match trends
  • Awards Section: View all achievements and milestones
  • PDF Reports: Export comprehensive progress reports
How do I view activity details?

Tap any activity card to see its full details:

  • From Dashboard: Tap any activity in the "Recent Activity" section
  • From Training Tab: Tap any activity in the list

The Activity Detail screen shows:

  • Complete activity information (date, duration, type, notes)
  • Performance rating and focus areas (for training)
  • Match statistics (score, goals, assists, positions played)
  • All attached photos and videos in a grid view
  • Quick actions to Edit Activity or Add Media
What are Soccer Facts?

After logging a training session or match, you'll see a fun educational soccer fact! These facts cover:

  • History: Origins of soccer and famous moments
  • Rules: Interesting facts about the game rules
  • Famous Players: Legends of the sport
  • World Records: Amazing soccer achievements
  • Tips & Skills: Training tips related to your focus areas
  • Fun Facts: Surprising trivia about soccer

The app remembers which facts you've seen and shows you new ones each time!

üîß Troubleshooting

I don't see my training session after saving. What happened?

First, check these locations:

  • Go to the Dashboard and scroll to the Activity Timeline
  • Navigate to the Training tab
  • Refresh the screen (pull down to refresh)

If your activity is still missing, please contact support with details about what you tried to log.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! Junior Kickers Journey works completely offline:

  • No internet connection required
  • All data is stored on your device
  • All features work without Wi-Fi or cellular data
  • You can track activities anywhere, anytime
Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device:

  • Nothing is uploaded to the cloud
  • Nothing is sent to our servers
  • Data stays in the app's private storage on your device
  • You have complete control over your data

Important: If you uninstall the app or lose your device without backup, your data will be lost. We recommend backing up your device regularly through iCloud (iOS) or Google Drive (Android).

How do I delete data?

You can delete data in several ways:

  • Delete Individual Media: Remove photos or videos attached to activities (select media in Gallery tab and delete)
  • Delete Player: Go to Settings, tap the player, then delete (you can't delete your last player)
  • Delete All Data: Uninstall the app from your device

Note: Individual training sessions and matches cannot be deleted yet. This feature is planned for a future update.

Warning: Deleted data cannot be recovered. Please be careful when deleting.

The app isn't working on my device. What should I do?

Try these steps:

  1. Check Requirements: iOS 15.1+ or Android 7.0+
  2. Update the App: Make sure you have the latest version
  3. Restart Your Device: Close and reopen the app
  4. Check Storage: Ensure you have enough space on your device
  5. Reinstall: As a last resort, uninstall and reinstall the app (this will delete all data)

If issues persist, contact support with your device model and OS version.

Will I lose my data if I update the app?

No! App updates preserve all your data:

  • Training sessions remain intact
  • Match records are preserved
  • Achievements stay earned
  • Statistics remain accurate
  • Photos and videos are maintained
What device permissions does the app need?

Junior Kickers Journey requests minimal permissions:

  • Camera (optional): Take photos/videos directly in the app
  • Photo Library (optional): Select existing photos/videos from your gallery

You can deny these permissions and still use all other features. The app does not request or need internet, location, contacts, or any other permissions.
