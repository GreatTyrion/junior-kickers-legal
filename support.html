Getting Started

Junior Kickers Journey is available on both major platforms:

üì± iOS: Download from the App Store (iOS 15.1+ required)
üì± Android: Download from Google Play (Android 7.0+ required)

Simply search for "Junior Kickers Journey" in your app store and tap Install. The app is free to download and use.

No account needed! Junior Kickers Journey requires no registration, no login, and no email. Simply download and start tracking immediately.

All your data stays on your device. This privacy-first approach means you're in complete control of your child's information.

When you first launch the app, you'll be guided through creating a player profile:

  1. Enter your child's name
  2. Select their date of birth
  3. Choose their primary soccer position(s)
  4. Tap "Create Profile"

That's it! You're ready to start tracking activities. The whole process takes less than a minute.

Yes! Junior Kickers Journey supports multiple players:

  • Go to Settings ‚Üí Add Player
  • Create additional profiles for each child
  • Switch between players anytime from Settings
  • Each player's data is kept separate and private

The Dashboard and all statistics automatically update when you switch players.

Features & Usage

Logging a training session takes less than 2 minutes:

  1. Navigate to the Training tab
  2. Tap "Add Training Session"
  3. Select the date and time
  4. Enter duration (e.g., 60 minutes)
  5. Choose activity type (Practice, Skills, Fitness, Scrimmage, etc.)
  6. Select focus areas (Passing, Shooting, Dribbling, etc.)
  7. Rate performance using the 1-5 star buttons
  8. Add notes (optional)
  9. Tap "Save"

After saving, you'll see a fun soccer fact! If you've unlocked any achievements, you'll celebrate those too.

Recording a match is simple:

  1. Navigate to the Training tab ‚Üí "Log Match"
  2. Select date and time
  3. Enter opponent name and select home/away
  4. Enter team and opponent scores
  5. Record your child's statistics (goals, assists)
  6. Select position(s) played
  7. Enter minutes played
  8. Rate performance (1-5 stars)
  9. Add notes (optional)
  10. Tap "Save"

Match statistics contribute to achievements like "First Goal" and "Hat Trick Hero"!

Tap any activity card to see its full details:

  • From Dashboard: Tap any activity in the "Recent Activity" section
  • From Training Tab: Tap any activity in the list

The Activity Detail screen shows:

‚úì Complete activity information (date, duration, type, notes)
‚úì Performance rating and focus areas displayed as tags
‚úì Match statistics (score, goals, assists, positions)
‚úì All attached photos and videos in a grid view
‚úì Quick actions to Edit or Add Media
Tip: Activities with attached media show a üì∑ icon on their cards, making them easy to identify at a glance.

You can edit any activity after logging it:

  1. Find the activity on your Dashboard or Training tab
  2. Tap the activity card to open the Activity Detail screen
  3. Tap the "Edit Activity" button
  4. Make your changes to any fields
  5. Tap "Update Training Session" or "Update Match"
Note: The app will recalculate achievements based on the updated data. If an achievement no longer qualifies (e.g., reducing goals from 3 to 2 removes "Hat Trick Hero"), you'll be notified.

Adding media is easy from the Activity Detail screen:

  1. Tap an activity card to open the Activity Detail screen
  2. Tap "Add Media" button
  3. Choose "Take Photo/Video" or "Select from Gallery"
  4. Select or capture your media
  5. Media is automatically attached to the activity
Storage Tip: Photos are automatically compressed to save space while maintaining quality. Original files in your phone's gallery remain unchanged.

When you unlock an achievement, you'll experience a celebration!

Replay anytime! Go to Progress ‚Üí Awards, tap any earned achievement, and select "Replay Celebration" to see it again.

The app has 4 main tabs for navigation:

First, check these locations:

  • Go to the Dashboard and look at the "Recent Activity" section
  • Navigate to the Training tab to see all activities
  • Pull down to refresh the screen

If your activity is still missing, please contact support with details about what you tried to log.

You can delete data in several ways:

  • Delete Individual Media: Select media in the Gallery tab and delete
  • Delete Player: Go to Settings, tap the player, then delete (you can't delete your last player)
  • Delete All Data: Uninstall the app from your device
Warning: Deleted data cannot be recovered. Please be careful when deleting.
